About Our Zoo

WildLife Zoo is dedicated to wildlife conservation and education. We provide a safe environment for animals and a fun learning experience for visitors. WildLife Zoo merges ethical conservation with immersive education across 50 acres of naturalistic habitats. Home to 450+ species, we lead global survival programs for endangered wildlife. Every visit supports our mission: protecting biodiversity and rehabilitating animals, ensuring a sustainable future where humans and the natural world thrive together.
